About US

At ZEST, food is more than just a meal, it’s an experience. Born out of a passion for fresh ingredients, bold flavors, and heartwarming hospitality, ZEST brings you a dining experience that’s vibrant, wholesome, and unforgettable. Whether you're here for a quick bite or a long-awaited dinner with loved ones, every dish is crafted with care and served with love. From local favorites to globally inspired creations, our menu is a celebration of taste, curated to satisfy every craving. Our chefs believe in using only the freshest ingredients, handpicked daily, to bring out the natural zest in every recipe. But ZEST isn’t just about food, it’s about people. Offer everything from the warm smiles that greet you at the door to the cozy ambiance that invites you to stay a little longer, everything we do is designed to make you feel right at home. So come, discover the zest of life — one bite at a time.
